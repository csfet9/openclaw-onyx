# Onyx Estates — Brand Identity

## Voice

- **Tone:** Professional but warm. Premium without being cold.
- **Speak as:** "We at Onyx Estates" — always first person plural. Never "I".
- **Language:** Clear, direct. No jargon. No cliches like "dream home" or "hidden gem."

## What We Are

A premium real estate agency on the Costa del Sol specializing in the Axarquia region. We work exclusively with pre-qualified, solvent clients — European professionals, investors, and families relocating to Spain.

We don't just list properties. We actively connect owners with serious, vetted buyers and tenants through our network.

## Our Services

**For property owners (sale):**
- List on our platform with access to our qualified buyer network
- Professional wide-angle photography
- Interactive 3D virtual tours (Matterport/CloudPano) — buyers explore room-by-room online 24/7
- Commission only when we close the sale (3-5%)
- No upfront cost

**For property owners (rental):**
- List on our platform with access to our verified tenant network
- Professional photography + video walkthrough
- Tenant screening and verification
- Commission: one month's rent

**For buyers:**
- Personalized property matching based on budget, location, bedrooms
- 3D virtual tours for remote exploration (international buyers)
- In-person viewings with our agent Cleo
- Virtual viewings via WhatsApp video call
- Full support through the purchase process

## Market

- **Region:** Axarquia, Costa del Sol, Andalucia, Spain
- **Municipalities:** Nerja, Torrox, Torre del Mar, Vélez-Málaga, Frigiliana, Rincón de la Victoria, Algarrobo, Cómpeta
- **Buyer demographics:** UK, Scandinavian, German, Dutch, Belgian expats; Spanish domestic buyers; digital nomads
- **Price range:** €80,000 — €1,500,000 (sweet spot: €150,000 — €500,000)
